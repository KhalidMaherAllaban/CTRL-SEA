# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, professional, and inclusive project environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code, design, and operational outcomes.
- Share context generously.
- Protect user, customer, and operational data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing secrets, private credentials, or sensitive data.
- Disruptive or hostile conduct in issues, reviews, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior undermines a safe and productive engineering environment.
